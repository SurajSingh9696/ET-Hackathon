# AI Money Mentor - Impact Model

## Executive Summary

AI Money Mentor addresses the critical gap in financial literacy and personalized financial planning for Indian retail investors. By democratizing access to intelligent financial analysis, we enable millions of Indians to make better financial decisions and improve their wealth creation trajectory.

## Problem Statement

### Current Challenges in India

1. **Financial Literacy Crisis**
   - Only 27% of Indian adults are financially literate (S&P Global FinLit Survey)
   - 76% of Indians don't understand basic investment concepts
   - Result: Poor financial decisions, suboptimal portfolio allocation

2. **Lack of Personalized Advice**
   - Financial advisors charge ₹5,000-50,000 for comprehensive planning
   - Only accessible to HNI segment (top 5%)
   - Middle class (95% of investors) left with generic advice

3. **Analysis Paralysis**
   - 8+ hours average time to manually analyze portfolio performance
   - Complex calculations (XIRR, asset allocation) require Excel expertise
   - Most investors never evaluate their actual returns

4. **Suboptimal Tax Planning**
   - ₹46,800 average tax savings opportunity missed per person
   - 68% of taxpayers don't fully utilize Section 80C
   - Result: ₹23,400 crore in excess taxes paid annually (estimated)
