## VISUAL PRODUCTION NOTES

### Must-Show Screens
1. **Problem Stats** (10s): Large text with numbers
2. **Logo + Homepage** (5s): Clean branding
3. **Live Demo** (1:10):
   - Form filling (fast-forward with highlights)
   - Results dashboard (pause on key metrics)
   - Zoom on AI recommendations
4. **Impact Chart** (25s): Animated bar/line charts
5. **Closing Screen** (15s): Links + call-to-action

### Pacing
- **Fast**: Problem setup, form filling
- **Medium**: Solution intro, impact explanation
- **Slow**: Results walkthrough (let viewers read key numbers)
- **Dynamic**: Quick cuts for demo, hold on impact

### Audio
- Upbeat background music (25% volume)
- Clear voiceover, professional tone
- Sound effect on key reveals (health score, XIRR)

---

## SCRIPT VARIATIONS

### If Running Long (Cut to 2:50)
Reduce impact section by 10 seconds - remove technology mention, keep just individual + scale impact.

### If Running Short (Extend to 3:10)
Add 10 seconds to demo showing the asset allocation pie chart and tax opportunities section.

---

## EQUIPMENT RECOMMENDATIONS

- **Screen Recording**: OBS Studio (1080p, 60fps)
- **Editing**: DaVinci Resolve or Adobe Premiere
- **Audio**: Blue Yeti or Rode microphone
- **Presenter**: Clean background, good lighting
- **Music**: Epidemic Sound or Artlist

---

## DELIVERY TIPS

1. **Narration**: Enthusiastic but not rushed, clear enunciation
2. **Demo**: Cursor highlights (yellow circle) on key numbers
3. **Transitions**: Quick cuts, no long fades
4. **Text Overlays**: Bold, large font for key numbers
5. **Branding**: Logo watermark (bottom right, 20% opacity)
